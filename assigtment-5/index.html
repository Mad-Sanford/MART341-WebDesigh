Background

Born in Montana, surrounded by artists, I have always been in a sea of creative energy that has supported my love for production. As a person with dyslexia, I picture things metaphorically and symbolically, giving me a unique perspective on our reality.

Medium

I use many different mediums, such as clay, acrylics, and charcoal. I believe that working in a single medium is restrictive to my artistic process. Each idea manifests in its own individual style.

Inspiration

I pull inspiration out of all moments, from what I experience with anything and everything outside of myself all the way to the blissful and despairing moments alone in my mind.
